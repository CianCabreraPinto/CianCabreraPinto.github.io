About Me

Hi, I'm Cian Cabrera-Pinto, and I'm a prospective UX/UI Designer from Waterford. I am currently undertaking an undergraduate degree in Interaction Design at the University of Limerick. I chose this course because it integrates graphic design, visual design, research, and user testing. I have enjoyed designing interactive systems that work well for end users through my studies. I plan to create more websites for local companies that are having a hard time with their online presence. I aspire to expand my business to reach the wider community by assisting individuals and organizations in creating their personal brands.

People Who Inspire Me

Chris Do inspires me through creativity by opening my eyes to the possibilities of freelance and creative media. Through his business website and YouTube channel, The Futur, he has offered hundreds of free tutorials and information that have been a goldmine to my growth.

Virgil Abloh, the former creative director at Louis Vuitton and creator of Off-White, shows me that I should think differently and view things from different perspectives. His mantra, "If the student is a candle, I could put this candle in an all-white gallery space it looks like a piece of art. If I put this candle in a garage it looks like a piece of trash," encourages me to always question and reinterpret the world around me.

Don Norman, the famous father of UX/UI Design, has made me curious to know more about how visual and virtual objects like websites are created and interpreted by the end users.
